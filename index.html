Facts and FAQs

Let's get things straight.

Skipping one workout ruins everything.

One missed day won’t matter. What counts is showing up again the next time.

How often should I work out?

Start with 3–4 days a week, giving your muscles time to rest and recover between sessions.

Lifting is only for men.

Strength training benefits everyone — it builds confidence, posture, and overall health.

How long before I see progress?

Most beginners notice progress in 4–8 weeks — stay consistent and it will show.

You should lose or gain weight before starting.

You can start training at any stage. Exercise helps your body adapt — whether you’re aiming to tone up, gain strength, or lose fat.

Do I need supplements right away?

Not at all. Focus on real food, sleep, and consistency — supplements can come later.
